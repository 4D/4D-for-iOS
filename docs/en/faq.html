FAQ

What are the prerequisites and requirements to use 4D for iOS?
    To use 4D for iOS you need to satisfy prerequisites like installing latest Xcode version on your mac, using 4D Developer Pro / 4D Server v17 R2 64-bit and activate the 4D Mobile Service.

How do I choose an Apple Developer Program?
    Enroll in the Apple Developer Program will allow you to test your app in real devices and deploy your app in App Store or in-House depending on the Program you choose.

How do I create a free Apple Developer account?
    It is pretty easy to create a free Apple Developer account to test your app on your iPhone.

How do I install my app on my device?
    In 4D for iOS you can easily test your application on your iPhone in a few steps

How do I limit data to specific queries?
    This version doesn't allow you to embed more than 10 000 records in your app.

    Future versions will allow you to apply filters to display only the information you need in your app.

How do I modify and update my records?
    This version allows you to build read-only apps.

    Future versions will allow you to add and edit your records right from your app and synchronize your data with the server.

How do I include related tables to my app?
    This version allows you to build a List form and a Detail form for each table.

    Future versions will allow you to access fields from related tables to build your views.
